# Research Plan

## Research Questions

- What resume formats and workflows are most useful for local-first job-search assistance?
- Which job posting fields are reliable enough to drive tailoring?
- Which compensation and company-review sources are useful without over-weighting noisy anecdotes?
- What application tracking features are worth building before integrations?
- What privacy boundaries are required before public packaging?

## Source Categories

- Job postings supplied by the user.
- Company career pages.
- Public compensation sources.
- Employee review sites.
- Public professional profiles and company pages.
- Resume/ATS guidance from reputable career sources.

## Research Method

- Date-stamp every external claim.
- Keep URLs with summaries.
- Label confidence and source quality.
- Separate market facts from assistant recommendations.
- Prefer small focused research passes over broad crawling.
